To Kill A Mockingbird: The Game

This game was made for my 9th grade English teacher.

I made a game for one of my school projects ealier that year, and he said he had always wanted one of his students to make a game version of To Kill A Mockingbird.

It was an awesome feeling to hear him call it "legendary" and he remains one of my favorite teachers that I've ever had.
