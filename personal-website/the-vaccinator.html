The Vaccinator

This game was made in 36 hours for a high school hackathon with the theme being COVID where it won Best Overall Project.

Unlike all my other games, this was developed in a team of three people where I worked on the programming, another worked on the art, and another worked on a required presentation for the hackathon.

This was my first hackathon and I was extremely pleased with the experience, and I look forward to working in more team environments like that in the future.
